# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics – Foundations, Systems, and Applications

A compact technical book introducing the fundamental concepts of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics for beginners.

This book explores the intersection of artificial intelligence and physical systems, covering the foundations of embodied cognition, the technical systems behind humanoid robotics, and real-world applications of these technologies.
