# References for Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

1. **Siciliano, B. & Khatib, O.** (2016). *Springer Handbook of Robotics*. Springer. This comprehensive handbook covers fundamental robotics concepts including humanoid robotics systems and control.

2. **Pfeifer, R. & Bongard, J.** (2006). *How the Body Shapes the Way We Think: A New View of Intelligence*. MIT Press. This book explores embodied cognition and how physical interaction shapes intelligence.

3. **Schaal, S.** (1999). Is imitation learning the route to humanoid robots? *Trends in Cognitive Sciences*, 3(2), 233-242. This paper discusses learning mechanisms relevant to humanoid robotics.

4. **Brooks, R.A.** (1991). Intelligence without representation. *Artificial Intelligence*, 47(1-3), 139-159. A foundational paper on behavior-based robotics and physical AI.

5. **Hodgins, J. & Kunii, T.L.** (1992). Animating human walking. *Proceedings of Graphics Interface*, 69-78. This research covers human locomotion and its applications to humanoid robotics.

6. **Khatib, O. et al.** (2018). Human-centered robotics: From conceptual design to real-world validation. *IEEE Robotics & Automation Magazine*, 25(4), 24-33. This paper discusses human-robot interaction principles.

7. **Turing, A.M.** (1950). Computing machinery and intelligence. *Mind*, 59(236), 433-460. The foundational paper for artificial intelligence and machine intelligence evaluation.
